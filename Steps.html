Ingredients and Method

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • Oil
  • Magic sarap
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Tomato

Steps to do this Menu:

      1. Heat the pan
      2. If the pan is hot already, put the 2teaspoon of oil
      3. Put the sliced onion and tomato
      4. Put the egg and mix it
      5. Add salt and Magic sarap
      6. Put the egg in the Plate
